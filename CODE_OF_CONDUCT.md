# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other characteristic.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Use inclusive language.
- Accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Focus on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Discriminatory remarks or actions.
- Personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and appropriate action will be taken.
